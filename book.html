About the Book

Tensor Cookbook provides a comprehensive guide to understanding and utilizing tensor networks in various applications. It covers fundamental concepts, practical examples, and advanced techniques, making it suitable for both beginners and experienced practitioners.

Whether you are a researcher, student, or professional, this book will equip you with the knowledge and tools to effectively work with tensor networks.
